About Us

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) represent dynamic domains within Computer Science and Information Technology, dedicated to crafting intelligent computers and systems capable of performing tasks previously reliant on human intelligence. As AI and ML seamlessly integrate into our daily routines, their transformative potential is continuously escalating. Various sectors, including banking, finance, manufacturing, automobile, healthcare, and cybersecurity, are witnessing a proliferation of opportunities in the realm of AI and ML. The global AI software industry experienced a remarkable 54% growth in 2020, with projections indicating a consistent annual expansion of over 40% until 2024. These statistics underscore the undeniable brightness of the future for AI and ML. Students enrolled in this program will acquire a skill set that positions them as innovators, empowering them to make significant contributions to humanity on a broader scale.
